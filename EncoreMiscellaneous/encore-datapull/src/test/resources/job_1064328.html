CRNA Needed for Locum Tenens Coverage at Busy Hospital in Northern Ohio

Date posted: Today Location: OH

Job description

A busy hospital is seeking a CRNA for locum tenens coverage.
The facility is located in northern Ohio.
The locum will work 7:30 am-5 pm with no call requirements. Cases are general bread and butter and OB - the CRNA must be able to place epidurals. Supervision is light. EMR system is Centricity and there will be a brief orientation on the first morning of the assignment.
